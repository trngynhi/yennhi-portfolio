FILM PHOTOGRAPHY

Moments that Stayed

ABOUT THE PROJECT

Film photography has been an ongoing personal practice since late 2020, beginning with a point-and-shoot camera inherited from my great-grandmother. Self-taught through experimentation, the process has since expanded across different cameras, with film images remaining a meaningful and integral part of my creative journey. Over time, this practice has grown into a way of observing, documenting, and engaging more deeply with everyday moments.
