BRAND IDENTITY

Vịt Kafé

ABOUT THE PROJECT

Vịt Kafé is a freelance brand identity project for a takeaway café, centered around a playful and memorable visual language. The project focuses on menu design as the primary application, supported by logo variations, typography, and a cohesive color palette. Built around a minimal duck character holding a cup of coffee, the identity aims to be friendly, approachable, and easily recognizable.
