ABOUT THE PROJECT

D6 Gymnasium is a 2D illustration exploring texture and atmosphere through stippled grain shading. Inspired by a space closely tied to personal memory, the piece captures a familiar gymnasium in its renewed state, balancing nostalgia with subtle transformation.
